Common Questions

How long does it take to confirm an appointment?

We typically confirm appointments within 24 hours of your booking request. For urgent healthcare needs, please call our emergency line.

Can I reschedule or cancel an appointment?

Yes. Please contact us at least 24 hours before your scheduled appointment if you need to reschedule or cancel.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes. All information submitted through our forms is encrypted and protected by healthcare privacy regulations including HIPAA compliance.

What if I don't see my service area listed?

Contact us directly. We may be expanding our services to new areas or can provide virtual consultations outside our service regions.
