Welcome to Care Connect Health

Care Connect Health is a premier healthcare provider headquartered in Ottawa, Canada, dedicated to delivering exceptional nursing and wellness services to under served communities across the region. Our primary service focus is comprehensive in-home nursing care, combined with virtual consultations and personalized wellness programs that bring professional healthcare directly to our clients' homes and communities.

Founded with a vision to bridge healthcare gaps in rural and urban communities alike, Care Connect Health emerged from the recognition that many Canadians face significant barriers to accessing quality medical care. Over the past decade, our organization has grown from a small team of dedicated nurses to a multi-disciplinary healthcare provider serving thousands of patients throughout Ottawa and surrounding areas.

Our experienced professionals, including registered nurses, nurse practitioners, and health educators, provide evidence-based in-home nursing services encompassing wound care, chronic disease management, post-operative care, medication management, and preventive health screenings. Beyond traditional nursing, we offer virtual care consultations that connect patients with healthcare professionals from the comfort of their homes, ensuring accessibility for those with mobility challenges or busy schedules.

Additionally, our comprehensive wellness programs address nutrition, physical activity, stress management, and health education to empower individuals to take control of their health. Care Connect Health represents a commitment to compassionate, accessible healthcare that honors the dignity of every patient and recognizes the unique health needs of diverse communities across Ottawa and beyond.
